Who Am I?

and some stuff about me.

You may be wondering, who is this madman and why am I so captivated by his work?

Well as you may not know, I am Emmet Allen, a 20-something-year-old with nothing to prove other than the fact that the earth is an oblate spheroid.

But in all seriousness, I started this blog as a way to vent and connect to those who may not be able to openly discuss how they feel about certain aspects of life, but in a way that puts some humility into it. It’s not meant to be taken too seriously, hence the blog title, instead, it’s a place to have an introspective chuckle about some of the things that go on in life.
